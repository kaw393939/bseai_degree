## Keep The Discussion Active

The BSEAI Discord is the easiest place to keep this conversation moving after
the presentation ends.

Use it to:

- continue the faculty discussion around the degree
- review the handouts and supporting evidence
- share critiques, additions, and implementation ideas
- coordinate collaboration around the B.S. in Enterprise AI

## What To Expect

The server is read-only by default for new joiners; request access to the
faculty channel when you arrive. Expect draft material, short video briefs,
and ongoing revisions to the handouts linked from this site — not a chat
stream.

## Why It Matters

The proposal gets stronger if the conversation does not end in the room.
Faculty need a place to compare concerns, test arguments, share examples, and
keep the degree connected to real institutional questions.
