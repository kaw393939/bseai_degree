## Keith Williams

**Senior University Lecturer, New Jersey Institute of Technology.**
Author of the B.S. in Enterprise AI. Twenty-three years teaching at the
university level. A working practitioner who uses AI to build every
day.

## Twenty-Three Years in the Classroom

Keith has spent his career teaching at NJIT across the arc of modern
computing — through the rise of the web, mobile, cloud, data, and now
AI. The BSEAI degree is the synthesis of that arc: a curriculum built
by someone who has watched four waves of technology pass through higher
education and seen which pedagogical habits survived each one and
which did not.

The degree was approved through the university's normal curriculum
process. Its first cohort — 100+ majors — enrolls in Fall 2026.

## A Working Practitioner

BSEAI is not a degree written by someone who has read about AI. It is
written by someone who builds with it daily. Recent and ongoing
systems:

- **[Studio Ordo](https://studioordo.com)** — a governed,
  enterprise-grade AI platform with chat, workflow automation, hybrid
  retrieval, deferred jobs, evaluation harnesses, release evidence,
  publishing, and admin control in one compact system. The working
  proof of the kind of system BSEAI graduates are trained to build and
  run.
- **A zero-copy Apple-silicon RAG system** built on the local Apple
  LLM released over Christmas 2025. Native on-device retrieval
  pipeline demonstrating what enterprise AI looks like when data never
  leaves the machine.
- **An ACES CG rendering engine** — color-managed scene-referred
  rendering pipeline for production-grade visual work.
- **Automated quality-assurance loops for multimedia content** —
  evaluation harnesses that hold generative media output to
  professional standards.
- **A FITS data renderer for JWST imagery** — scientific data pipeline
  converting raw James Webb Space Telescope observations into
  publication-grade images.
- **Network-graph intelligence systems** — knowledge graphs over
  document corpora with live retrieval and reasoning.

These are not portfolio pieces. They are the daily work that taught
him what a BSEAI graduate actually needs to know.

## The Thesis Behind the Degree

> AI shifts economic value from skill to human quality. The
> curriculum has to shift the ratio — 40% skill, 60% judgment — or it
> is obsolete on delivery.

BSEAI is engineered around that inversion. Skill is the carrier wave;
human formation is the signal. The eight studios are designed to build
both — in the order and at the cadence students can actually absorb.

## Why Him, Why This Degree

Keith has been using ChatGPT since its first public week and agentic
coding since the field first called it agentic. He built Studio Ordo
as one proof. BSEAI is the other. The two are connected: the degree
trains the kind of practitioner who can build and run systems like
Studio Ordo inside real organizations.

The degree is a first draft. Its second draft will be written with
humanities and business colleagues across NJIT, with peer institutions
launching their own programs in 2027 and 2028, and with employers who
hire the first graduating cohort in 2030.
