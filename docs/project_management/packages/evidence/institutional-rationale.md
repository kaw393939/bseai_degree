# Evidence Packet: BSEAI Launch Readiness and Institutional Rationale

## 1. Purpose

To establish that the BSEAI degree is launch-ready for Fall 2026 because NJIT
already has the curricular spine, delivery habits, and institutional logic to
support it.

## 2. Audience Takeaway

NJIT already has the pedagogical pipeline and technical capacity to deliver this
degree. The case should be made through present curriculum and launch readiness,
not through a retired-degree memorial.

## 3. Source Inputs

- Architect CV / Institutional History (`aboutme.md`).
- `docs/_original/research/bseai-degree-curriculum.md`.
- `docs/_original/foundation/bseai-degree-positioning.md`.
- `docs/_original/foundation/bseai-course-spine.md`.

## 4. Required Evidence or Source Constraints

- Must rely on concrete present evidence: course spine, catalog structure,
  capstone pathway, and deployable graduate outcomes.

## 5. Structural Outline

- **The Launch**: BSEAI is a Fall 2026 degree, not a speculative someday plan.
- **The Spine**: The existing course architecture already supports
  enterprise-AI formation.
- **The Proof**: Recent systems, analytics, UX, and enterprise-AI coursework
  map directly to deployable graduate outcomes.

## 6. Visual or Compositional Notes

- This data feeds the launch-context slide, the `why-bseai` page, and the
  `author-context` page.

## 7. Implementation Notes

- Emphasize that the degree launch is driven by market demand, curriculum fit,
  and institutional readiness. Do not make the retired WIS degree the headline.

## 8. Verification Criteria

- Does the packet make the Fall 2026 BSEAI launch feel concrete, credible, and
  academically defensible?
