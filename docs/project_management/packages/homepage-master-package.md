## 1. Purpose
This document governs the entire structural and visual sequence of the BSEAI pitch presentation. It acts as the definitive blueprint to ensure the deck functions as a tight, single rhetorical system.

## 2. Audience Takeaway
The presentation delivers a cohesive 3-act narrative: The Rupture, The Mechanics, and The System, proving the necessity of the BSEAI degree.

## 3. Source Inputs
- Phase 12 Page Packages.
- Phase 13 Evidence Packets.

## 4. Required Evidence or Source Constraints
- The slide sequence must remain bounded to 15-20 slides for a 15-minute presentation.
- Every assigned archetype must be natively supported by the Markdown rendering engine.

## 5. Structural Outline
### Act 1: The Rupture (Slides 1-5)
Establish the paradigm shift. Fast, highly visual, cinematic.
### Act 2: The Mechanics (Slides 6-12)
How do we adapt? Methodical, structured, evidence-heavy.
### Act 3: The System (Slides 13-17)
The Institutional Solution. Inspirational, authoritative, commanding.
