# Page Package: Why BSEAI?

## 1. Purpose

To provide the definitive, institution-level rationale for why the B.S. in
Enterprise AI (BSEAI) program exists, focusing on the gap between frontier
model research and organizational deployment.

## 2. Audience Takeaway

Organizations do not need more undergraduate researchers; they need deployment
engineers. The BSEAI studio spine is explicitly designed to fill this enterprise
deployment gap.

## 3. Source Inputs

- LinkedIn Labor Market Report 2026.
- WEF Future of Jobs 2025.
- `docs/_original/research/bseai-degree-curriculum.md`.
- `docs/_original/foundation/bseai-degree-positioning.md`.
- `docs/_original/foundation/bseai-course-spine.md`.

## 4. Required Evidence or Source Constraints

- Relies heavily on the `deployment-gap` and `institutional-rationale`
  evidence packets.
- Must cite specific course numbers (IS331, IS480, IS425) to map rhetoric to reality.

## 5. Structural Outline

- **The Deployment Gap**: Why training everyone to build foundation models is a
  mistake.
- **The Market Signal**: LinkedIn and World Economic Forum data on the rise of
  the Forward-Deployed Engineer.
- **The Studio Spine**: How the curriculum translates to capability.
- **NJIT's Comparative Advantage**: Why we are positioned to own the deployment
  space.
